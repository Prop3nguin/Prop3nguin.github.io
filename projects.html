  • Valtare - (Constructed language)

    A Common language used thoughout the Kingdom of Man but also appears in large open markets like Altenhiem.

    The language Valtare aka "Lordspeak" Was created by the people of Arranzale who's decendants once spoke modern English; traces of this Ancestry can be noticed within the dialect's structure.

    The number system Valtare uses is a duodecimal system, meaning there are 12 unique number symbols instead of 10, much like how binary uses only two, 1 and zero with "10" representing a value of two.
